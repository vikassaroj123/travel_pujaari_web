Refund Policy

At Travel Pujaari, we understand that sometimes plans change, and you may need to cancel or modify your bookings. Our refund policy is designed to be transparent and fair, ensuring that you receive appropriate compensation based on the type of booking and the timing of your request.

Cancellation and Refund Process

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Initiate Cancellation: Log into your Travel Pujaari account and navigate to the 'My Bookings' section. Select the booking you wish to cancel and follow the on-screen instructions to initiate the cancellation process.
  • Submit Refund Request: After cancellation, submit a refund request through the designated option provided in your booking details. Ensure all required information is accurate to avoid delays.
  • Processing Time: Refund requests are typically processed within 7-14 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your refund has been processed.

Eligibility for Refunds

The eligibility for refunds depends on the following factors:

  • Booking Type: Refunds are available for most bookings, including flights, hotels, and tours. However, certain non-refundable bookings, such as promotional offers or last-minute deals, may not be eligible for refunds.
  • Cancellation Timing: The timing of your cancellation plays a critical role in determining your refund amount. Cancellations made within the allowed time frame (as stated in the booking terms) may be eligible for a full or partial refund.
  • Supplier Policies: Refunds are subject to the policies of the service providers (e.g., airlines, hotels, tour operators). We will adhere to these policies when processing your refund request.

Partial Refunds and Non-Refundable Services

In certain cases, you may be eligible for a partial refund, depending on the services rendered:

  • Partially Used Services: If a service has been partially used (e.g., partially completed tours), a partial refund may be issued based on the unused portion.
  • Non-Refundable Charges: Certain fees, such as processing fees, service charges, and taxes, are non-refundable.
  • Third-Party Services: For bookings involving third-party services, the refund policy of the respective provider will apply. We are not responsible for the refund decisions made by third-party providers.

Refund Methods

Refunds will be issued using the original payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Refunds to credit or debit cards will be processed back to the original card used for the booking.
  • Bank Transfers: For payments made via bank transfer, refunds will be processed to the original bank account.
  • Wallet Refunds: If you used a digital wallet for payment, the refund will be credited back to the same wallet.
  • Gift Cards or Vouchers: Refunds for bookings made using gift cards or vouchers will be reissued in the form of a new gift card or voucher.

Exceptions to the Refund Policy

Please note the following exceptions to our refund policy:

  • Force Majeure: Refunds may not be available in the event of force majeure situations (e.g., natural disasters, political unrest) that affect travel plans.
  • Non-Refundable Bookings: Some bookings, such as non-refundable flight tickets or hotel rooms, are explicitly marked as non-refundable at the time of purchase.
  • Late Cancellations: Cancellations made after the allowed cancellation period may not be eligible for a refund.

Contact Us for Refund Assistance

If you need assistance with a refund, please contact our customer service team at support@travelpujaari.com. Provide your booking reference number and details of your request to expedite the process.
