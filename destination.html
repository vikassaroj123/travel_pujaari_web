    Various Travel Products For Agents On Travel Pujaari

    With Travel Pujaari, travel agents get access to a wide array of travel products and services and thereby enjoy high margins and fantastic profits.

    1. FLIGHTS

    • As a flight ticket booking portal for travel agents, travel agents can avail of exclusive deals on one-way and roundtrip flights on major domestic and international FSC and LCC carriers. Book flights originating outside India with Global (SOTO) fares and receive more earnings on each booking.
    • You can book different air carriers, be it domestic or international for different sectors using Sector Fares. The best B2B flight ticket booking portal for travel agents, Travel Pujaari, has a multitude of new features that make every booking a hassle-free experience for travel agents.

    2. TRAIN

    • Become an IRCTC Authorized E-ticketing Agent on our train booking portal, Riya Connect.
    • Search and book train tickets on our leading B2B train ticket booking portal for your customers. Get incredible returns on your train ticket bookings with our multiple rail products and registration options.
    • We have Rail tourism packages such as Bharat Gaurav JY, Wellness, Deluxe Train, Coach Tours and many more on Travel Pujaari.

    3. HOTELS

    • Choose from a rich bank of 1 million+ hotel properties worldwide and 1500+ direct contracted hotels on our hotel booking portal for travel agents.
    • Search hotel rooms by city, ratings, amenities, cancellation policy and price to offer your customers the best-in-class and budget-friendly hotel options.

    4. VISA

    • Travel Pujaari is one of the best visa portals for travel agents, as we provide all visa services under one roof. We have processed 1 million+ visas and have 4+ decades of experience in visa processing.
    • We have Authorized Visa Application (AVA) centers for Singapore in 7 cities (Mumbai, New Delhi, Jaipur, Chennai, Bengaluru, Pune, and Ahmedabad) and Malaysia in 3 cities (Mumbai, Chennai, and Delhi) across India. With our visa portal for agents, you can apply for e-visa online for UAE and Thailand without any hassles.

    5. BUS

    • On our bus ticket booking portal for travel agents, travel agents can filter & search from a wide range of bus routes. They can further filter by fares, arrival time, departure time, bus type & bus operators to sell bus tickets to your customers across India.

    6. TRAVEL INSURANCE

    • Travel agents can apply for travel insurance online that best suits the passengers' needs.
    • We offer insurance for Domestic Travel, Solo Trip, Business Travel, International Travel, Students, Multiple Trips, Leisure Travel, and Senior Citizens.
    • Choose our travel insurance booking portal for affordable premiums, excellent coverage, and a hassle-free claim process.

    7. CAR RENTALS

    • Get the best deals with us on our car booking portal for agents. With our car booking app for agents, you can choose airport transfers and local outing bookings for your trips.

    8. AIRPORT LOUNGE

    • Our agents can book their clients access to the most exclusive airport lounges across the globe with just a few clicks. Enjoy comfort, luxury, and relaxation in a serene environment before your flight.
