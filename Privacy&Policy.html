Privacy and Policy

At Travel Pujaari, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy and Policy statement outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We gather various types of information to deliver and enhance our services:

  • Personal Information: Includes your name, contact details (email, phone number), and business information collected during registration.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our website and services, including IP address, browser type, and visited pages.
  • Transaction Data: Details of your transactions, including payment information, booking history, and related interactions.
  • Technical Data: Data related to your device, such as operating system, and device identifiers.
  • Communication Data: Records of your interactions with us, including customer service inquiries and feedback.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is utilized for several key purposes:

  • Account Management: To set up, maintain, and manage your account on our platform.
  • Service Provision: To provide you with the travel services you request and to customize your experience.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your inquiries, resolve issues, and provide customer service.
  • Transactional Communications: To send you confirmations, updates, and notifications related to your transactions and bookings.
  • Marketing and Promotions: To send promotional materials, newsletters, and special offers, with your consent, if applicable.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and protect our rights and interests.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data with robust security measures:

  • Encryption: Sensitive data is encrypted using industry-standard encryption protocols during transmission and storage.
  • Access Controls: Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only, using secure authentication methods.
  • Security Audits: Regular security assessments and audits are conducted to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.
  • Data Backups: Regular backups are maintained to prevent data loss and ensure data recovery in case of system failures.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your data in the following circumstances:

  • With Service Providers: To facilitate our services, including payment processing, email delivery, and customer support. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data.
  • For Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of company assets, where your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity.
  • Legal Requirements: To comply with legal obligations, respond to lawful requests, or protect the rights and safety of our users and our company.
  • With Your Consent: In cases where you have provided explicit consent for data sharing.

Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

  • Access: Request access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data when it is no longer necessary for the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing under certain circumstances.
  • Objection: Object to the processing of your data based on legitimate interests.
  • Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format for transfer to another organization.

To exercise these rights, please contact our data protection officer at support@travelpujaari.com.

Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Significant changes will be communicated to you via email or through a notice on our website. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.
